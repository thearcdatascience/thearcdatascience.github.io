‘Arc’ symbolizes the shift that is happening in human lifestyle and environment - One from human assisted technology to technology assisted humans.


The Arc -the data science club of IGDTUW is a community of zealous individuals who will help in introducing the students of University to the rapidly growing world of data science early into their college journey.


The club intends to be the guiding force for earnest students to learn the various skills that come under the realm of data science, including but not limited to, pre-processing, analyzing and building predictive models.


We dream to provide the bare minimum skillset to each and every student of the university in data sciences to thrive and flourish in a data driven future by organizing data collection workshops, web scrapping seminars and datathons. Our coterie also offers modules on artificial intelligence, machine learning, data mining for enriching every mentee’s skills.
